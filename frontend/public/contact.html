Hi there, I'm Ben Cox

I made Domaniator for a few reasons. Once I had gained access to Verisign's daily .com zone updates ( A fun data set by the way, totally reccomend applying for access) I had a few issues in finding a constructive use for it.

Another thing that I had always wanted to do is to see what kind of HTTP headers are used on the web and where / why.

Using this tool I can figure out what persentage of the .com domains use Apache as the front facing servers, I can also figure out how many of them have P3P policies, A task that the ISC is doing as well:

The system is powered on the back my MySQL, Google Go and Lighttpd

The crawler attempts to fetch one page of every domain every 30 days. If you feel that I am being annoying by doing this please do let me know at: ben+jojo@benjojo.co.uk Any other queries can also go to that email address.
